FAQs

1. How accurate are astrology readings?

Astrology provides symbolic insights; exact predictions depend on reflection and openness.

2. Do I need to provide my birth time?

Exact birth time improves accuracy, but approximate times are still useful.

3. Can astrology predict my future?

It reveals trends and potentials, empowering you to make informed choices.

4. Will astrology tell me bad news?

Readings guide and advise; they don’t aim to scare or alarm.

5. How often should I get a reading?

Many clients do yearly or when major life changes happen.

6. Can I have an online reading?

Yes, online consultations are available via WhatsApp, email, or video call for global clients.

Astrology Tips

Align With Moon Phases

Use new moons for new beginnings and intention-setting; full moons are ideal for completion and reflection.

Know Your Rising Sign

Your rising sign shows how the world sees you — align presentation and timing to its influence for social and career benefits.

Track Mercury Retrograde

During Mercury retrograde, review and revise plans, back up communication, and avoid signing irreversible agreements when possible.

Use Astrology for Reflection

Daily journaling with zodiac prompts helps you identify cycles, emotional patterns, and growth opportunities.
